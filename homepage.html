About "THEGREENLAND"

"THEGREENLAND" is a fairly new Chenaurus Map server (about 1 year old now) and is fast gaining attention from the "DAYZ" PS4/PS5 community.

Our 36 slot server can be played by anyone from the "casual" once a week player
to the "hardcore" everyday gamer.

"THEGREENLAND" covers most aspects of gameplay for users who like to go on murderous rampages
on the coast or even for users who like to be seen as survivalist enthusiasts living alone in the woods.
