THEGREENLAND tips



























Every community server is unique and for that reason we have created this tips page
to list some thing you may find usefull on you adventure through THEGRENNLAND:

  1. All gunracks outside army tents will always either have a KA 101, M4A1 or MOSIN.
  2. TRUCKS spawn with building supplies.
  3. ALL cars and trucks spawn full.
  4. Try not to kill freshies give them a chance.
  5. Join our Discord for event information.
  6. You always spawn in fresh facing north.
  7. No sakra cars on this server.
  8. Priest infected always have chocolate and teddy bear.
  9. Safezone is in vybor industrial surrounded by green smoke.
  10. Active discord
  11. Killfeed on discord.
  12. You can build anywhere.
  13. Restart times are 5am/10am/3pm/8pm and 00am GMT.
  14. Bears travel in groups of two.
  15. First time on server gear bonus.
  16. Prisoner infected will have bacon or bandage on them mostly.
  17. Please doNOTuse alt accounts on this server. It not only ruins your game experience but most
    importantly other players. This is a rule not just a polite request. Anyone found using an alt or
    suspected of using an alt will be banned for two weeks. If you break this rule again it will be a permenant ban!!!!!!
