Events

Here on THEGREENLAND we like to keep the server fun and active thats why we have added a number
of exciting and challenging events.
These events can drop at any time, to get the latest news on when and where simply join our discord and check out the events channel.

Air Drops

This event has to be number one on the list as it will provide you with hard to come by loot and a pvp challenge.
As we are running this server on console we dont have the machanics to do anything to visual, so these airdrops can be seen on the players screen as red smoke coming out of a sea chest.
The loot contained within these chests is selected by random and will usually contain a gun, grenades or landmines ammo, food, medical equipment and clothing.
As mentioned above to get the most out of this event join our discord for more information on times and locations.























GOT A VSD, PLATE CARRIER, AMMO, BANDAGES AND NADES THANKYOU AIRDROP.

Fight Nights

FIGHT NIGHTS are a fantastic way to settle disputes between enemies or even friends.

Fight nights are an event that is held in the safezone so NO GUNS allowed, this creates a
safe environment for people to have fun and settle disputes in a controlled way.
Join the discord for information on when this event drops.

Pink Dress Hunt

This has to be the most fun event "THEGREENLAND" hosts.
A random town will be announced on the discord within this town there will be an N.P.C with a Pink
dress on, you must find and kill the N.P.C to obtain the loot he/she carries.
